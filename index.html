About Me

I'm an electronics undergrad from Vadodara, with an unhealthy obsession with propulsions, control systems, and hobbyist radio antennas. I specialize in neither, but honestly, I'm just trying to make sense out of my engineering degree.

Lately, I've been working on a model-rocketry project -- which involves PCB design, signal processing, GUI programming, and a pinch of aerospace engineering (only at the hobbyist scale).

In my spare time, I sleep... which in my opinion, separates me from core electronics engineers, who are obnoxiously sleep deprived!
